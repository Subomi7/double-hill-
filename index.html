Who are we

In short, Kefi is “the spirit of joy”, which is no coincidence to our name. Although we’re aware that everyone has their own unique meanings of joy, we also know that it always comes down to freedom. Our core mission is to help create that freedom, thus helping you “find your Kefi”.
